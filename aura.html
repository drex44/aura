The Problem

The Exposure Gap

Education focuses on technical skills, but career growth depends on interaction. For first-gen achievers, this lack of exposure creates a glass ceiling.

Career Success Drivers

Research indicates the vast majority of long-term career success is attributed to soft skills, yet training investment is inverse.

High Friction

The Stake
